Hello! My name is David Lawson and I am currently working as a Research Assistant at the Center for Brain Circuit Therapeutics at Brigham and Women's Hospital. I hold a bachelor's degree in psychology from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst, as well as a minor in sociology and a certificate in social work and welfare.

My research interests focus on the development and evaluation of neuromodulatory interventions for psychiatric illness, as well as tackling social barriers that limit the accessibility of these specialized care options. I have also spent several years working in research on neurodegenerative illness, including dementia (MCI, Alzheimer's, DLB, PSP, etc), Parkinson's Disease, Essential Tremor, and Dystonia.

I am currently exploring programs to pursue a PhD in Clinical Psychology where I hope to continue my work.
